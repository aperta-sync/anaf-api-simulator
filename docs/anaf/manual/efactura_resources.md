# ANAF e-Factura Official Resources

The `e-Factura` project represents a fundamental step in the digitalization of the Romanian Ministry of Finance. It replaces traditional invoicing communication paths with a national centralized platform.

## Main Portal
[ANAF RO e-Factura Overview](https://www.anaf.ro/anaf/internet/ANAF/despre_anaf/strategii_anaf/proiecte_digitalizare/e.factura)

## Useful Information Provided by ANAF
- Clarifications for using the national electronic invoice system RO e-Factura
- Announcements regarding the registration in the e-Factura RO forced executions register.
- Necessary information for taxpayer compliance with legislative changes.
- Additions to the clarifications on obligations established by Article II of Law No. 139/2022.
- General communications and B2G clarifications.
- Constant updates regarding the implementation trajectory towards an exclusively B2B framework.

## Key Official Guides
- **General Usage Guide:** An end-to-end tutorial addressing how business users and administrators should utilize the system.
- **Dedicated Tax Codes & VAT Categories Guide:** A comprehensive technical guide assigning specific codes to types of invoices, exemptions, and VAT rules inside the national billing system.

## e-Factura Project Subsections
- **B2G (Business-to-Government):** The original mandate requiring businesses operating with state agencies to report all invoices electronically.
- **B2B (Business-to-Business):** The ongoing extension (starting 2024 heavily) to report entirely private transactions.
